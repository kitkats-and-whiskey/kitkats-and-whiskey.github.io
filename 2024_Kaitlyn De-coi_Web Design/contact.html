How to Book a Table at Lilly Pilly

We are excited to have you dine with us! To ensure you have a delightful experience, please follow these simple steps to book a table.

Visit our booking page (you're already one step down), click on our email lillypillycafe@gmail.com and head on over to step two

Select your preferred date and time and our lovely staff will let you know the availability of the time slot, make sure to note that we aren't open public holidays

Provide your details including your name, contact number, and email address, alongside the number of guests and any special requirements they may need (high chair, wheelchair acess etc.)

Review your details and double check to make sure everything's correct and hit send!

You will recieve confirmation with your booking details via email or sms if a phone number was provided. Make sure to check your junk folder

Important info

Booking hours: We accept reservations from 9:00 AM to 8:00 PM every day

Reservation Policy: We recommend booking at least 24 hours in advance, especially for weekends and holidays

Cancellations: If you need to cancel or change your reservation, please do so at least 2 hours before your scheduled time. You can cancel or modify your booking via the link provided in your confirmation email or by calling us at +61 07 4943 1292

Group Reservations: For parties of 6 or more, please contact us directly to ensure we can accommodate your group comfortably

FAQ

Can I walk in without a reservation? Yes, we welcome walk-ins, but reservations are recommended to guarantee a table, especially during peak hours

What if I'm running late? Please call us if you anticipate being more than 15 minutes late. We may release your table if we do not hear from you

Do you offer outdoor seating? Yes, we have a lovely outdoor seating area. You can request outdoor seating when you make your reservation

Can I reserve a table for special occasions? Absolutely! Whether it's a birthday, anniversary, or any special event, let us know in advance, and we'll do our best to make it memorable
